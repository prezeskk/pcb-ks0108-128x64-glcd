## Design Rules ##

* Clearance: 8 mil
* Track Width: 20 mil
* Via Diameter: 27 mil
* Via Drill: 13 mil
* Zone Clearance: 8 mil
* Zone Min Width: 8 mil
* Zone Thermal Antipad Clearance: 8 mil
* Zone Thermal Spoke Width: 15 mil
